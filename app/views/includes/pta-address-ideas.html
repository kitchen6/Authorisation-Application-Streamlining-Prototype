Idea 6: change the order of screens in the change your address journey

The current order is:

  • Do you get tax credits?
  • Do you live in more than one place?
  • Find your address
  • Edit your address (optional)
  • When did you start living here?
  • You cannot use this service to update your address

This is 6 steps to find out that you cannot do it. If the first or second question is something like ‘When did you change address?’ you could get people to where they need to go faster.

Idea 7: let people tell us an earlier date online

It would be good if this could happen in real time but, if not, send the change to a digital mail queue. Tell them it will take a few days to update instead of sending someone to the helpline.

Idea 8: remove hover on main address and postal address cards
